Our Philosophy

Flavour is the
true language
of memory

At OSSUA, every dish is a conversation between season and place. We source our ingredients exclusively from local artisan producers — the herbs from our rooftop garden, the fish from the morning boats of Sestri, the truffle from a farmer whose family has worked the same forest for six generations.

Chef Marco Delacroix leads our kitchen with obsessive precision and boundless creativity. His tasting menus evolve with every harvest, telling the story of the land and sea through textures, temperatures, and flavours you will carry with you long after the meal is over.

Marco Delacroix
Head Chef & Founder, OSSUA

The Setting

Designed for intimacy

Our 36-seat dining room is an exercise in restraint. Warm candlelight, hand-woven linens, handmade ceramic tableware crafted exclusively for OSSUA by artisan potters in Oaxaca. The room speaks softly so the food can speak loudly.

Every table at OSSUA offers complete privacy. We seat no more than eight tables per service, ensuring that each guest receives the full attention of our team — from the moment they arrive to the moment they leave.

We ask that you arrive 15 minutes before your booking to enjoy a welcome aperitif in our lounge, where our sommelier will guide you through the evening's wine programme.
